Javascript Card Game

Javascript, HTML, CSS. A card appears and the player has to click on the corresponding shape or keypress to match to the card, they have 30 seconds to click as many as possible. If they select the wrong answer, a sound is played and they are locked out of the game for a set period of time. We added extra features, such as a trick card, and a streak feature for which a player receives 2 seconds added to their time if they score 10 in a row. We also used Velocity.js to animate the instructions panel.
