Mayden Finance Calculator

Typescript, SCSS, HTML. For this task I created a finance calculator, for intended use by prospective Mayden Students. You enter the amount you wish to borrow, the expected salary and monthly repayment percentage. It then calculates the total repayment amount, total number of months it will take to repay, and the total additional admin fee to pay on the borrowed amount.
